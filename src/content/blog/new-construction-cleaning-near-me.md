## What Is New-Construction Cleaning?

New-construction cleaning removes drywall dust, debris, and film left after building or remodeling. It is different from a normal house tidy because fine dust settles on every horizontal surface, inside cabinets, and on fixtures.

## Rough Clean vs Final Clean

**Rough clean:** after major construction dust, before final finishes — floors, fixtures, and accessible surfaces so trades can finish.

**Final clean:** before owner or tenant move-in — detailed pass for presentation and walkthroughs.
