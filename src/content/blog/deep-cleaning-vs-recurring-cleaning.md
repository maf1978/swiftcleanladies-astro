Deep cleaning and recurring cleaning solve different problems. Deep cleaning is a detailed reset for buildup, appliance interiors, baseboards, and overlooked corners. Recurring cleaning keeps kitchens, baths, and living areas on a steady schedule so you are not starting from scratch every visit.

Many homes do a deep clean first, then move to weekly or bi-weekly care. Busy weeks and guest arrivals often fit better as a [one-time cleaning](/services/one-time-cleaning/) in a fixed 2 to 5-hour slot with two professionals.
