## What Affects House Cleaning Prices in Crawfordville?

Cleaning prices change with how long a two-person team needs to work your checklist. Larger homes, more bathrooms, heavier buildup, and pet hair all add minutes. Frequency also matters: a maintained biweekly home is usually faster than a first visit after months without help.

**Main cost drivers:**

- Square footage and number of bedrooms
- Number of bathrooms
- Weekly, biweekly, or monthly schedule
- Condition at the first visit (maintenance vs. reset)
- Pets and shedding
- Add-ons (oven interior, fridge interior, organization)
