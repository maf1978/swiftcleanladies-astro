A little prep helps the visit stay productive. Clear floors and counters of laundry, toys, and papers. Secure pets if needed. Note gate codes, parking, and any rooms that are off-limits.

Share priorities before the crew arrives: kitchens and baths first, guest rooms, or office high-touch points. For offices, confirm after-hours access so the schedule limits disruption. For new builds, confirm whether you need a rough clean or a final pass.
